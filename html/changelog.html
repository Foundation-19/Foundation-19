05 June 2022

EgorDinamit updated:

  • IPCs play an additional sound when moving.
  • Added radial menus from /tg/station. Currently used when choosing cyborg module icon and organ manipulation surgeries.

01 June 2022

EgorDinamit updated:

  • Human inventory UI(when you drag&drop) now uses browser popup. It looks better, yes.
  • Events have slightly different chances to run. "Nothing" has significantly lower chance.
  • You can reboot APCs without unlocking them first.

25 May 2022

EgorDinamit updated:

  • Laughing and clapping emotes now provide a sound effect.

23 May 2022

EgorDinamit updated:

  • Slippery motor oil is now available to emagged janitor cyborgs.
  • Water/Lube stun durations are heavily nerfed so you won't spend 10 seconds lying on the floor.
  • The wet floor overlays don't become invisible every once in a while now, making it easier to spot a wet/lubed floor.

22 May 2022

BayStation 12 updated:

  • Added holomaps.
  • Added rotating alarm lights.
  • Gibs from gibber no longer fly at mach 5 speed.
  • Viscerators from the grenade get user's faction.
  • Fax machines can now be emagged and don't require access nor have cooldown.
  • Leaping with broken legs might damage you. Be careful! It also reduces the distance you leap at.
  • Added venomous snakes and changed leeches sprites.
  • Frag grenades deal more damage.
  • Laser damage falls off with distance.
  • Added meteor build mode.
  • OFD resprited.

  • The angels are coming! Apostle is back in action.
