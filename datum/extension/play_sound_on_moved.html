/datum/extension/play_sound_on_moved

This extension allows a movable atom to play a sound for each tile it is moved.

Vars

always_playIf TRUE, the movement sound will always play. If FALSE, it will only play when dragged over hard surfaces (i.e. not space and carpets).
atom_holderA reference to the atom affected by this extension.
move_soundsA list of paths to sound files. When atom_holder is moved, one of these will play, picked at random.
move_volumeThe volume of the sound to be played.

Procs

DoMoveSoundPlays a random sound from move_sounds at volume move_volume, centered at the turf of atom_holder.
